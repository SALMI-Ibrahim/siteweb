Ocean Freight


1. Full Container Load (FCL) Services

Our Full Container Load services offer you the flexibility to book an entire container for your exclusive use. This is an ideal option for businesses with large shipments or those requiring additional security and control over their cargo.


2. Less Than Container Load (LCL) Consolidation:

For smaller shipments, our Less Than Container Load services allow you to share container space with other shippers, reducing costs and providing a cost-effective solution for businesses with smaller cargo volumes.


3. Specialized Cargo Handling:

We specialize in handling a diverse range of cargo. Our team ensures that your specialized cargo is handled with the utmost care and compliance.
